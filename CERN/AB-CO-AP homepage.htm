Application section goal
    A service-oriented Applications section capable of :
     . Supporting the current Accelerators control system.
     . In charge of providing the application software for the control of PS, SPS, and LHC, in collaboration with OP.
     . Responsible for providing software services & competence to CO, OP, and Equipment groups.
Missions
    Development and Operational Environment and Tools.
    Generic components for OP and Equipment groups.
    Development, Support & Maintenance of the Operational applications in collaboration with OP members.
    Development of the application software for LHC, in collaboration with OP members.
    Software Standards and Development Guidelines.
    Support and knowledge in software development technologies and methods.
